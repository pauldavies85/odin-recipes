Lassagne

Description

Ingredients

  • 500g Coles Australian Beef Mince
  • 700g jar Coles Italian Tomato & Basil Passata
  • 6 Coles Lasagne Sheets
  • 490g jar bechamel sauce
  • 1 cup (120g) Coles shredded pizza cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C. Heat a large non-stick frying pan over medium-high heat. Add mince and cook, stirring with a wooden spoon to break up any lumps, for 5 mins or until the mince changes colour. Add the passata and stir to combine. Bring to the boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 10 mins or until the mince mixture thickens slightly.
  2. Spread one-third of the mince mixture over the base of a 6-cup (1.5L) ovenproof dish. Top with 2 lasagne sheets, trimming to fit if necessary. Spread with one-third of the bechamel sauce and sprinkle with one-third of the cheese. Make 2 more layers of mince mixture, lasagne sheets, bechamel sauce and cheese.
  3. Bake for 35 mins or until golden brown and cooked through. Set aside for 5 mins to cool slightly.
